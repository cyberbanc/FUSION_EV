M9 FUSION EV 1.3.6.6 — NEGATIVE EV OFF, $10 / $15

CHANGES
- LOW_NEGATIVE_EV $5 trading removed.
- selected EV < 0 is always NO_TRADE.
- 0 <= selected EV < 0.05 keeps the $10 MID_EV stake.
- selected EV >= 0.05 keeps the $15 HIGH_EV stake.
- Payout, shadow recent, quality and cooldown rules are unchanged.
- PostgreSQL history and paper bank are preserved.
- New stake_mode: nonnegative_ev_10_15.

SAFETY
Even when TRADE_FILTER_ENABLED=false, negative EV cannot create a trade because
stake_for_ev returns eligible=false and stake=0.
